Baby Feeding Diary App – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 23, 2025 · Effective Date: September 23, 2025

We respect and protect user privacy and are committed to safeguarding your data.

1. Data Collection and Use

  • Local Storage: Feeding, sleep, stool and other records are stored only on your device (CoreData) and are not uploaded to any server.
  • No Personal Identity Data: No registration or login is required. We do not collect names, ID numbers or contact details.
  • No Advertising SDKs: We do not share your data with third parties.

2. Membership Purchases

Payments are processed via App Store In-App Purchases by Apple. We do not access or store your payment information.

3. Permissions

The app does not request sensitive permissions by default. System capabilities are invoked only when you choose to export or share data.

4. Data Security and Risk Notice

All data is stored locally. The developer is not responsible for data loss due to device loss, damage or system reset.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for parents/guardians and not directly for children. We do not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time and takes effect upon publication in the app or on this webpage.
